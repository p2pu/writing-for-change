Welcome to Writing for Change

Thanks for joining this learning circle! Over the course of the next five weeks, the readings and activities in this course will help you to: - Identify your motivations and desired impact in facilitating change - Collectively develop interventions that positively impact your communities - Explore methods that invite iterative collaboration and generative criticism

We hope the members of your cohort can support one another long after the conclusion of this learning circle. To the future!
